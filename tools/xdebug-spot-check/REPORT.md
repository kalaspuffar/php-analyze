## Known limitations

- **Xdebug's own per-call overhead inflates the reference durations.**
  Xdebug instruments every call; its `t_out − t_in` includes its own
  bookkeeping. The relevant comparison is per-call shape, not absolute
  nanosecond-for-nanosecond agreement.
- **PHP-specialised internals (`strlen("hi")`, etc.) are invisible to
  the Zend observer** per `COMMENTS.md` C-5. The Xdebug-only footnote
  above lists any that appeared on this run.
- **Position-wise pairing assumes deterministic call order.**
  Non-deterministic fixtures (timing-, randomness-, or IO-dependent)
  will produce a misleading timing-delta histogram. The canonical
  `recursive_walk.php` fixture is deterministic.
